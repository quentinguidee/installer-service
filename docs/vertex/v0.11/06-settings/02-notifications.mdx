# Notifications

In this menu, you can setup a Discord webhook to receive notifications about your server.

## Setup

To setup a webhook, you need to create a webhook in your Discord server.

1. Go to your server settings, then to the "Integrations" tab, and click on "Create Webhook".
2. Choose a name and a channel for your webhook, then click on "Copy Webhook URL".
3. On Vertex, paste the URL in the "Webhook" field, and click on "Save".
